FONTLOG for the Arsenal font

This file provides detailed information on the Arsenal 
font Software.

This information should be distributed along with the 
Arsenal fonts and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information

http://www.ukrainian-type.com/home/

Arsenal is a Unicode typeface family that supports languages that use the Latin, and Cyrillic scripts and its variants, and could be expanded to support other scripts.

More specifically, this release supports the following Unicode
ranges: Latin-1, Vietnamese, GF Cyrillic Pro

2011 v.1.000 (Andrij Shevchenko)
- Original Latin (except pan-African, and Asian) and Cyrillic (except Asian 
languages) were designed by Andrij Shevchenko.

October 17, 2016 v.2.000 (Cyreal + Nhung Nguyen)
- Cyrillic expansion to GF Cyrillic Pro by Cyreal and Vietnamese expansion by Nhung Nguyen.

June 26 2017 v.2.001 (Alexei Vanyashin)
- Fixed  #7 — lack of diacritic in Ҋ U+048A, ҋ U+048B, placement of dot in ỵ U+1EF5.


To contribute to the project contact Alexei Vanyashin at a@cyreal.org
